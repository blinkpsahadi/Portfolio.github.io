Here are the most interesting one that I decided to include on my Portfolio.

Drug Oversight & Control DRUGOC-project

DrugOC is a pharmacovigilance web application designed to achieve two main objectives: 1. Determine whether a drug is responsible for one or more adverse effects during a treatment. 2. Report these adverse effects to the appropriate authorities through comprehensive reports. The process follows the French causality assessment method: [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23773347].
